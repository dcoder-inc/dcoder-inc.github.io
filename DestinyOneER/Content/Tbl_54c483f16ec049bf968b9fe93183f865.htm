biz_process 
Definition  
Specification table describing business processes (workflows), the path and systems used in the linked flow of activities with a specific start and finish that describe a process. Each row represents a different process, including: Cancel/Run with Low Enrolment Request, Cheque Requisition, Course Section Cancellation, Course Section Information Update, Course Section Proposal, Second Section Openings, Submitted Student Grading, Transaction Processing, Wait Listed Course Section Opening, Send Message. Processes are composed of tasks, described in BIZ_PROCESS_TASK
