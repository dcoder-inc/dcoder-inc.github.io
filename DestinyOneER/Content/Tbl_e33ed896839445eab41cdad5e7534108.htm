certificate 
Definition  
An award for the successful completion of a specified number of units, or completion of mandatory and elective courses in an academic program. This table contains both certificate templates and instances of certificates associated with students. CERTIFICATE_TYPE is 'school' for a template (and STUDENT_ID is null)
